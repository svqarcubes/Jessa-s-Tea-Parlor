    About Us

    Hey y'all welcome to the beautiful city of Woodstock!
    We are so blessed to have y'all stopping by. We may be small, but we have big hearts, and everyone is family here at Jessa's Tea Parlor. When you walk in, we hope it brings you back home where you can relax over tea and spend much needed quality time with family and friends.

    We offer a wide variety of yummy loose leaf teas, sandwiches and desserts! We also love to support our small businesses, and use many local farmers all around Cherokee county. Oh, and don't forget to check out all the pretty things we have for sale throughout our place. Now come relax and enjoy time with family and friends.

    Tea Room Etiquette and Rules

    We are by reservation only, please use the form below, or call 404.551.9391!
    Currently booking Saturdays months in advance. We would love to have you during the week! Tuesday through Friday by reservation.

    • All unruly children with be given a shot of expresso and be sent home.
    • No sharing of tea pots or stands.
    • No outside food or drink.
    • Groups of 6 or more will require a non-refundable $50 Deposit upon booking a reservation. Gratuity will also be added for groups of 6 or more.
    • We are not Celiac safe but do offer gluten free items.
    • Please inform us of any allergies upon booking your reservation, we want you to have the best time!

      • Thank you so much for understanding, We cant wait to meet you!
